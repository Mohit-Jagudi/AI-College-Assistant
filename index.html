About GJITM

Where Knowledge Meets Opportunity

Established in 1997, Gyan Jyoti Institute of Technology & Management is a premier institution affiliated with IKGPTU, Jalandhar. Located in the education hub of Mohali, we offer an exceptional academic environment that fosters innovation, leadership and entrepreneurial spirit.

Our state-of-the-art campus is equipped with modern labs, a well-stocked library, sports facilities, and vibrant student clubs — ensuring holistic development beyond the classroom.
